About MedKart

MedKart is a student-developed medicine e-commerce platform built at Vidarbha Institute of Technology, Nagpur under the guidance of Prof. Aditya Patil. Our goal is to make essential medicines accessible to everyone — especially individuals in remote areas or with limited mobility — through a convenient, secure online experience.
